- A brief history of the NERDS BRAND -

Since 1983, NERDS candy has always been fun and innovative, with perfectly imperfect shapes that are better together. From the iconic dual-chamber packaging design to the chewy-crunchy texture in Ropes and Big Chewy NERDS, there's nothing else quite like them.
